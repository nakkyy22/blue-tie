What kind of wines does Blue Tie produce?

Blue Tie Wines is a modern and innovative wine company that creates distinguished wines along with the distinct concept. Today under the name of Blue Tie Light, Young and Fresh wines are produced

Why choose “Blue Tie”?

Along with the emotional values, Blue Tie Wines creates high quality products. We aim to create a trustworthy Partner in the form of wine, that will deliver sophisticated tastes and inspire Elegance and Courage. Taste the Confidence and commit to Excellence

Definition of the Logo

The logo of Blue Tie Wines is derived from the symbols of the Tie and Lion. The symbol of the blue colored Tie is a direct representation of the name of the brand and at the same time References all of the values of these wines. the Logo becomes complete with the Symbol of Lion, because of its resemblance to solidity, Empowerment and pride.
